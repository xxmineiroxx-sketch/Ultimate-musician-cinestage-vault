## What these products do well
- Setlist-first live flow with fast song reorder and arrangement edits.
- Section jump + loop while playback is running.
- Transition support (fade and smooth flow between songs/sections).
- MIDI control and mapping for hands-free performance.
- Cloud/team sync for setlists and arrangements.

## Implemented in Ultimate Musician now
- Rehearsal waveform authoring with grid (`BAR/BEAT/FREE`) and markers.
- Launch quantization policy (`IMMEDIATE/BEAT/BAR`) saved on arm.
- Transition policy (`CUT/CROSSFADE/OVERLAP`) saved on arm.
- Auto transient marker detection from waveform peaks.
- Loop markers + role-based stem restrictions transferred to Live.
- Live view showing quantized jump targets and transition preview.
- 12-note pad area with kit import and kit cycling.
